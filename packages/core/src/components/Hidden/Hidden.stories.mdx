# Hidden

Hidden is used to responsively show and hide the children component based on either `js` or `css` implementation.

```tsx
<Hidden down="S" implementation="js">
    <Button variant="solid" fullWidth>
        Will hide the children at and down of the breakpoint.
    </Button>
</Hidden>
```
